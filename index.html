Our Story

The Best Coffee in North Jersey, Right Here on Ringwood Avenue

Ringwood Ave Roasters started with a simple idea: the best coffee shop in north Jersey should be right here on Ringwood Avenue.

We're a family operation — roasting on-site, sourcing with intention, and putting down roots. Every bag of coffee we sell donates $1 to a local cause, because the neighborhood we're in should feel the difference. Our 5–11 Club was built for the early risers and regulars who make a coffee shop feel like home — unlimited morning coffee, a cup that's yours to keep, and membership in something we're building together.

We're in Haskell. We're staying.
